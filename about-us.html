About us

Globe Infinite is a European based company with an ethos of Loyalty, integrity and professionalism at its core. Founded by Arthur Bury Globe Infinite is a recently formed organization that has established a fresh approach to meet the needs of globally integrated personal and corporate security. Our youth as a company is underpinned by years of individual experience in the security industry across a variety of sectors. We have harnessed these experiences and as the volatility of our world has changed we have adapted, we take a very proactive approach, we plan with care remaining flexible throughout our service delivery. Our strategy is to bring together the vast experience of trusted associates across the globe and deliver truly specialized, focused, support to meet the security challenges of tomorrow as well as today.
